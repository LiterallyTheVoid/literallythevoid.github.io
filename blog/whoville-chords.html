Whoville Chords

I was seeing a lot of videos that were being using Whoville by Tyler, The Creator as background music. I thought this was a really pretty and minimal chord progression, so I decided to tab it out. 

        Notation for barre chords: position (roman numerals)/strings.
        Barring 5 strings on the second fret would be noted II/5
